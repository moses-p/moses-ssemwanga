About me

Building digital infrastructure for impact

I'm Moses Ssemwanga, a full-stack developer and IT Lead based in Kampala, Uganda. Currently heading all digital development at AIN Visibility Media, I specialise in building scalable, content-rich web platforms — from responsive frontends to secure, well-architected backends.
