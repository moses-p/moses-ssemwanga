About me

Turning data into decisions

I'm Moses Ssemwanga, a detail-oriented Data Analyst based in Kampala, Uganda. With over 3 years of experience transforming raw data into actionable insights, I work across the full analytics pipeline — from data collection and cleaning to modelling, visualisation, and storytelling.

Currently at AIN Visibility Media, I've designed automated data pipelines using Python and Apache Airflow, built Tableau and Power BI dashboards that reduced reporting time by 60%, and conducted A/B tests that improved client ROI by an average of 35%. I also developed and deployed a PostgreSQL data warehouse integrating payment APIs (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money) for consolidated transactional reporting.

I hold a BSc in Information Systems & Technology from Makerere University (2024), an Advanced Diploma in Data Science with R from Alison (2024), and a CCNA certification.
